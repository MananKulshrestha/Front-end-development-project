Basketball

Basketball is a fast-paced game played by two teams of five players on a rectangular court. The objective is to shoot a ball through a hoop 18 inches in diameter and 10 feet high mounted to a backboard at each end.

Basic Gameplay

Teams score by shooting the ball through the opponent's hoop. A regular field goal is worth two points, while a shot made from beyond the three-point line is worth three points. Players advance the ball by bouncing it while walking or running (dribbling) or by passing it to a teammate.
