Hockey

Hockey is a term used to denote various types of both summer and winter team sports which originated on either an outdoor field, sheet of ice, or dry floor such as in a gymnasium. The most popular forms are Field Hockey and Ice Hockey.

Basic Gameplay

Two teams play against each other by trying to maneuver a ball or a puck into the opponent's goal using a hockey stick. It is a highly demanding physical game known for rapid transitions between offense and defense.
