Interactive Course Learning

Cricket

Cricket is played between two teams of eleven players each on a field with a 22-yard pitch.

  • Batting: Scoring runs by hitting the ball.
  • Bowling: Delivering the ball to dismiss batsmen.
  • Fielding: Stopping the ball to prevent runs.

Football

Football is played between two teams of eleven players with the objective of scoring goals.

  • Goalkeeper: Protects the goal.
  • Defender: Stops attacks.
  • Midfielder: Links defense and attack.
  • Forward: Scores goals.
